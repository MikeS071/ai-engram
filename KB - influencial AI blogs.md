# Influential AI, Data, and Content Blogs

Here are 12 highly influential blogs with original ideas and strong followings in AI, Data, and Content:

## 1. The Batch by Andrew Ng

**Website**: [deeplearning.ai](https://deeplearning.ai)

- Weekly AI newsletter with original perspectives on AI trends, applications, and industry analysis
- Massive following from Andrew Ng's reputation as AI pioneer
- Focuses on practical AI implementation and societal impact

## 2. Lil'Log by Lilian Weng

**Website**: [lilianweng.github.io](https://lilianweng.github.io)

- Deep technical posts on ML/AI research with unique explanations
- OpenAI's VP of Research & Safety
- Known for making complex AI concepts accessible with original insights

## 3. Eugene Yan's Blog

**Website**: [eugeneyan.com](https://eugeneyan.com)

- Applied ML in production, data science, and ML systems
- Real-world case studies and original frameworks
- Popular among ML practitioners for practical, thoughtful content

## 4. Stratechery by Ben Thompson

**Website**: [stratechery.com](https://stratechery.com)

- Tech strategy and analysis, covers AI's business implications
- One of the most successful independent tech analysts
- Original frameworks like "Aggregation Theory"

## 5. Not Boring by Packy McCormick

**Website**: [notboring.co](https://notboring.co)

- Tech, business strategy, and emerging tech (heavy AI coverage)
- Extremely popular newsletter with 100k+ subscribers
- Known for deep dives and connecting disparate ideas

## 6. Dan Koe

**Website**: [thedankoe.com](https://thedankoe.com)

- One-person business methodology and knowledge monetization
- 196,000+ newsletter subscribers
- Focus on deep work, personal development, and "future-proof" skills
- Known for anti-niche philosophy and synthesizing ideas across disciplines

## 7. Chip Huyen's Blog

**Website**: [huyenchip.com](https://huyenchip.com)

- AI engineering, MLOps, and building generative AI platforms
- Focus on bringing AI into production with practical insights
- Covers real-time ML, data distribution shifts, and ML systems design
- Popular among ML engineers and practitioners building production systems

## 8. Sebastian Ruder's Blog

**Website**: [ruder.io](https://ruder.io)

- Natural language processing and multilingual AI research
- Research Scientist at Cohere, formerly at Google
- Covers LLM evaluation, instruction tuning, transfer learning, and modular deep learning
- Highly respected in the NLP research community

## 9. Andrej Karpathy's Blog

**Website**: [karpathy.github.io](https://karpathy.github.io)

- Deep learning fundamentals and neural network architectures
- Formerly Director of AI at Tesla and founding member of OpenAI
- Known for clear explanations of complex concepts (CNNs, RNNs, reinforcement learning)
- Influential voice in AI education and research

## 10. Simon Willison's Blog

**Website**: [simonwillison.net](https://simonwillison.net)

- AI tools, experiments, and practical LLM applications
- Co-creator of Django framework, builder of Datasette
- Daily explorations of new AI capabilities and tool development
- Essential reading for developers working with AI APIs and building AI-powered tools

## 11. Naval's Blog

**Website**: [nav.al](https://nav.al)

- Philosophy on wealth creation, happiness, decision-making, and life principles
- Founder of AngelList, prominent tech investor and philosopher
- Covers epistemology, agency, recruiting, product thinking, and personal development
- Known for dense, timeless wisdom distilled into accessible formats
- Popular podcast and tweetstorms on building wealth and finding happiness

## 12. The Innermost Loop by Dr. Alex Wissner-Gross

**Website**: [theinnermostloop.substack.com](https://theinnermostloop.substack.com/)

- Computational theory, AI, physics, and complex systems
- Research scientist focusing on AI, machine learning, and computational intelligence
- Explores fundamental questions at the intersection of physics and artificial intelligence
- Known for work on causal entropic forces and computational theory of intelligence
- Deep technical insights on AI theory and advanced computational concepts
