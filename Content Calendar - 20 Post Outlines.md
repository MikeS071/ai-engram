## Publishing Strategy

### Frequency Options

**Option 1: Twice Weekly (10 weeks)**
- Monday & Thursday posts
- Allows time for thoughtful expansion into full posts
- Best for maintaining quality and engagement

**Option 2: Daily (20 business days / 4 weeks)**
- One post per weekday
- Aggressive content push for rapid audience building
- Requires batch creation workflow

**Option 3: Weekly (20 weeks / 5 months)**
- Deep dive into each outline
- Maximum quality, depth, and engagement per post
- Best for building authority

### Content Development Workflow

1. **Select outline** from calendar
2. **Expand core paradox** into hook (3 variations)
3. **Develop transformation arc** into narrative
4. **Convert actionable steps** into concrete, tactical advice
5. **Craft memorable closing** that ties everything together
6. **Format for platform** (character limits, visual breaks)
7. **Add visual elements** where appropriate
8. **Schedule and publish**

### Platform-Specific Adaptations

**Twitter/X:**
- Lead with core paradox as thread hook
- 8-12 tweet threads
- Heavy use of line breaks and visual formatting

**LinkedIn:**
- Longer-form (800-1200 words)
- Professional framing
- More context and examples

**Newsletter:**
- Full expansion (1500-2000 words)
- Personal stories and deeper examples
- Clear section breaks

**Medium:**
- Deep dive format (2000-3000 words)
- Rich formatting and visual elements
- SEO optimization
